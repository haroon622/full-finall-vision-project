Safety Page

Welcome to our safety page! This page is designed to provide you with important information and resources to help you stay safe online.

Online Safety Tips

  • Never share your personal information with strangers online.
  • Be cautious when clicking on links or downloading attachments from unknown sources.
  • Use strong and unique passwords for all of your online accounts.
  • Avoid using public computers or public Wi-Fi to access sensitive information.
  • Report any suspicious activity or harassment to the appropriate authorities.

Resources

  • Cybersecurity.gov
